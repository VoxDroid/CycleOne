Privacy First. No Clouds.

CycleOne was built with one goal: to protect your cycle data from everyone, including us.

1. Local Storage Only

All your data (dates, symptoms, moods) is stored exclusively on your iPhone using Core Data. We do not have any servers, and we do not use iCloud sync.

2. No Accounts

You don't need to sign up or provide an email. Your data is tied to your device, not an identity.

3. No Ad-Tracking or Analytics

We do not use any third-party SDKs, ad-trackers, or analytics tools. We have no idea how often you use the app or what you log.

4. Your Data, Your Choice

You can export your data as a CSV file at any time. You can also delete all data from within the app, or by deleting the app from your device.

5. Third-Party Services

CycleOne does not connect to any third-party services. No network requests are made by the app. Zero dependencies on external servers or APIs.

6. Data Retention

Your data exists only as long as the app is installed. Uninstalling CycleOne permanently removes all data. We cannot recover it for you.

7. Children's Privacy

CycleOne is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy, reach us via our GitHub repository: github.com/VoxDroid/CycleOne
